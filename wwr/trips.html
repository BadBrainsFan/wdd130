Our Incredible Trips

The Grand Canyon Expedition

Embark on a multi-day journey through the majestic Grand Canyon. This trip is for the truly adventurous, combining thrilling rapids with breathtaking scenery and starry nights.

The Salmon River Family Float

A perfect trip for families and beginners. Enjoy a gentle float down the stunning Salmon River, with plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife spotting.

The Upper Animas Thrill Ride

For experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush. Navigate challenging Class IV and V rapids on the legendary Upper Animas River.
