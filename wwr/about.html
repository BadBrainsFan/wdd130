At Splash White Water Rafting, our mission is to provide exhilarating and safe white water rafting experiences that create lasting memories for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in connecting people with the beauty and thrill of nature, fostering a love for the outdoors, and ensuring every trip is an unforgettable journey down the river.

History

Founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river guides, Splash White Water Rafting started as a small operation with a big dream: to share the unparalleled excitement of whitewater with everyone. From our humble beginnings with just two rafts and a handful of intrepid explorers, we have grown into a premier rafting company, renowned for our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and delivering truly epic adventures. Over the decades, we've navigated countless rapids, witnessed incredible wildlife, and helped thousands discover their inner adventurer. Our journey has been one of constant learning and improvement, always striving to enhance the rafting experience while preserving the pristine beauty of the rivers we call home.
